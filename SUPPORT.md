# Support

For help with SendSwap, contact:

- Email: support@sendswap.io
- Website: https://sendswap.io/support
- Telegram: https://t.me/SendSwap_io
- X: https://x.com/SendSwap_io

## Best way to contact support

Email is the best channel for swap-specific support requests.

## Include this information

To help the team investigate faster, include:

- the asset pair used
- approximate time of the swap
- the deposit transaction hash, if available
- the payout or destination address
- screenshots of the status page or error message, if relevant

## Do not include

Do not send:

- private keys
- seed phrases
- exchange passwords
- unrelated personal documents

## Response scope

Support can help with:

- status questions
- delayed payout investigation
- route or quote questions
- basic troubleshooting

Support may not be able to override external network delays or third-party execution conditions beyond SendSwap's direct control.
